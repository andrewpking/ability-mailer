Ability Weekly Social 2023-2024

We are excited to announce we will be offering a weekly mixer at the Diversity and Access Lounge in The Allen Building (CSE1 202). This will be every Monday at 4:30pm during the academic quarter.

You are invited to join us to hangout, experience kinship, meet other students, and have fun.

Our first mixer will be on Monday, October 2nd, 2023.

Ability remains committed to securing accommodations with advance notice. We ask that accommodation requests are submitted 3 weeks in advance of an anticipated event by contacting our Adviser, EJ Pinera epinera@cs.washington.edu, so we can have enough time to ensure all attendees can engage in our programming. If you have any need for recurring accommodations, please submit your request and we will work with you to ensure your needs can be met.
